Skills and Jobs

If there really is a group of venture consultants who decide to invest in our project this time, this is really something that we feel very happy and partly look forward to. Because this project is a very ambitious project, if the project is to have a chance to continue to grow and succeed, it will need a strong enough human capital investment to make progress. bigger. From the moment I started getting serious about the project, I was always thinking about positions that I could fill when the opportunity arises. Now, when the opportunity has come, I decided to conduct a selection of 4 positions for the job with the following specific information:
AI Engineer

+ Skills Required:

- Able to communicate and use English fluently

- Sociable personality, ability to work in a team is a great advantage

- Have a progressive spirit and creative thinking

- Having a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or IT is a big plus

- Have a deep knowledge of math, probability, statistics, and algorithms.

- Have in-depth knowledge and experience in the field of AI (from 3 to 5 years of experience)

- Having skills and experience in using Python, Java, C++ programming languages ​​(3 - 5 years of experience).

+ Job Description:

- This project is an AI project and AI also plays a huge role so this position is extremely important. This position will be the person in charge of the main role in the project, designing the algorithm and how it works for AI. AI needs to be able to make high-precision decisions because these are issues related to human health.

- Presenting and giving periodic reports for the whole team to understand the progress of the work.

User Interface Design

+ Skills Required:

- Able to communicate and use English fluently

- Sociable personality, ability to work in a team is a great advantage

- Have a progressive spirit and creative thinking

- Having a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or IT is a big plus

- Having creative thinking, having a knack for aesthetics and fine arts is a huge advantage.

- Know how to use and have experience with graphic design tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD to come up with eye-catching and distinctive images to create highlights for their products (from 1-3 years of experience).

- Quickly grasp new trends to create new products.

- Ability to absorb, update and innovative thinking.

+ Job Description:

- This is a project on health application with the general user being the majority of people, even those who are not familiar with technology. Hence the need to create a user interface that is as simple and user-friendly as possible.

- Communicate and consult feedback from customers and associates to ensure quality and continuously refine to achieve high aesthetics.

- Attend meetings to discuss and write reports for the whole team to know the progress of the work.

Database Manager

+ Skills Required:

- Able to communicate and use English fluently

- Sociable personality, ability to work in a team is a great advantage

- Have a progressive spirit and creative thinking

- Having a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or IT is a big plus

- Have a solid technical and parallel knowledge base of different database structures.

- Having knowledge and experience in database administration (Oracle, etc) (2 - 3 years).

- Problem solving skills are required to assess situations and problems so that problems can be solved quickly when using a database.

- Must have skills and experience in using MySQL query language (1 - 3 years).

+ Job Description:

- This is an application that will target a large number of users, so the amount of data that needs to be stored is very large. The person in charge of this role has to stay focused and manage the huge data source that grows day by day.

- Design, install, configure and manage the company's database, ensuring that the database runs efficiently and without errors.

- Plan, monitor, and properly allocate disk, memory, network and other resources for the database.

- Backup and restore databases as needed under normal operating conditions and during failures.

- Communicate regularly with different audiences: end users, developers, managers, project teams.

Full Stack Developer

+ Skills Required:

- Able to communicate and use English fluently

- Sociable personality, ability to work in a team is a great advantage

- Have a progressive spirit and creative thinking

- Having a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or IT is a big plus.

- Can comfortably and confidently communicate with associates and customers.

- Need to understand Front-end technology and UI Design.

- Have skills and experience in using Python, Java, Ruby programming languages ​​(3 - 5 years of experience).

- Need to understand and have experience in Database, use MySQL, Oracle (2-5 years of experience).

- Service-oriented and object-oriented design concepts, including knowledge of data transfer objects and related design patterns.

+ Job Description:

- This will be the main overarching position and the bridge for the whole project team. The person in charge of this position will alternately work with all team members, directly supporting each position when facing difficulties or needing support.

- Synthesize the work and opinions of team members to ensure synchronization, avoid causing conflicts.

- In charge of supporting and developing parts with UI Design.

- Responsible for partial development and support together with Database Manager.

- In charge of supporting and developing parts together with an AI Engineer.
