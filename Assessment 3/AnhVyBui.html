Bui Anh Vy

UI/UX Designer

My nationality is Vietnamese and I live in Ho Chi Minh City. This is the second semester of my first year in RMIT. I'm Vietnamese and I was born in Ho Chi Minh City. I've lived in this city for 19 years. I don't travel often but I really love travelling. I want to go places that have delicious foods, beautiful scenery and fresh air. My hobby is watching and reading, but I prefer reading novels. I like outdoor activities such as badminton, swimming, rollerblading. I also like playing piano. My favorite pets are cats especially Munchkins.

IT interest

I choose IT because I feel it is very interesting with what we do with technology, I am not suitable to communicate with many people and like to calculate. Moreover, the IT industry has great job potential when graduating and has a good income. I became interested in computers when I was in 8th grade. At that time, I had to take a job exam to prepare for the exam when I finished grade 9 and computer science was a compulsory subject in my school. I studied Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Pascal and how to create a basic website. Then I found computer and science very useful and decided to choose IT when I applied for the university entrance exam.
