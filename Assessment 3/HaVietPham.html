IT interest

Despite having completed the Diploma of IT in Swinburne, IT is not my forte and I am not at all interested in IT. I am more interested in Computer science, which is in the making of games. Even without any interest, I am able to understand the importance of IT in Game Development and also what IT skills you can learn from Game Dev like:

  • Programming logic from making games,
  • Online tools and search engine used to research on the game or the process of making it,
  • Files and Directories, Compile from exporting games from the engine,
  • Flash memory, cloud storage and operating system compatibility from copying game files from systems to systems,
  • Debugging and software revisions from responding to count less errors during the development process.
