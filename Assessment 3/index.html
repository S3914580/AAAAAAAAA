Our Story

In recent years, the covid epidemic has spread everywhere, causing not only the economy but also the means of transportation to be delayed. In that situation, many people in remote places or in small provinces cannot go to the hospital for treatment. Seeing that difficulty, our team decided to make a medical app that can cure users with AI. AI has been widely applied in all fields and is a desirable technology in the future. Not only for treatment, we want users to be able to experience the convenience of AI and access technological developments around the globe.
As an RMIT student, we learn about information technology and what we learn will be applied to create a product that can help users during this epidemic. If possible, we would like to cooperate with many doctors and scientists to further improve the treatment of our app and users will feel secure to use AI technology in the treatment of diseases.
