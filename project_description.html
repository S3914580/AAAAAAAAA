2) Motivation


As everyone knows, the current Covid-19 pandemic situation is extremely stressful around the world and particularly in Vietnam. Although more than 2 years have passed, everything has not shown signs of ending, even the pandemic is likely to continue when recently, the number of infections spiked due to the Omicron mutation. Covid-19 has left extremely serious consequences in all fields, especially the medical field. Hospitals were all overwhelmed during the pandemic, which led to the fact that patients with other diseases also had difficulty in getting medical care for themselves. This leaves a lot of serious consequences, even the worst one, death. According to a statistic at the Ho Chi Minh City Dermatology Hospital, the number of medical examination cases here during the outbreak was only 700 cases/day, while this number on normal days is 2000-2500 cases/day.
After witnessing this heartbreaking situation, my team and I came up with the idea of ​​​​designing a medical application that people can use for online medical examination and treatment. This will be an extremely ambitious project because currently on the Vietnamese market, there is almost no product application similar to ours. And more importantly, if this project is successfully developed, it will be a turning point for today's healthcare industry in Vietnam. For example, in the context of Vietnam, the overload of front-line hospitals will be relatively solved, the crowded scenes will be almost reduced and appear less. Thereby helping to create both convenience for the patient and also help to protect the physical facilities and health of doctors and nurses. Not only that, our application also helps reduce the pressure on doctors by applying AI technology, one of the trends of IT in recent years, to support medical examination and treatment. AI will act as a virtual assistant to support doctors and can also examine patients with simple diseases to create a resting space for doctors.

3) Landscape


Currently, online medical examination and treatment is also gradually being noticed by people. In the world, a number of health applications as well as online forms of treatment have also been implemented to some extent, for example a few applications such as Doctor on Demand, LiveHealth Online Mobile, etc. These applications have been put into operation and obtained initial positive results. However, most applications with this function are not supported in Vietnam. Current health applications in Vietnam often focus only on storing electronic health records, monitoring daily health such as electronic health book applications, PC-Covid, or Samsung Health, Apple Health. Therefore, it can be said that the application field that we target is a relatively new thing in Vietnam, there will be not many competitors, perhaps just those who are just starting the project like us or some Startup teams. However, our application still has certain differences, this application will not only have medical examination and treatment in the style of regular video calls, but will also incorporate AI. AI will act as a virtual doctor as well as an effective assistant for doctors. We believe that the presence of AI will make a difference and blow a new wind into the field of health applications not only in Vietnam but also internationally.


Detailed Description


1) Aims


Our project is to develop a medical app using AI (Artificial Intelligence). Currently, on the market, there have been many types of medical apps appearing for the purpose of declaring vaccination information, treating diseases with doctors in local hospitals, etc. Countless such medical apps were born, and it is also of interest to many people. That is why we decided to make an AI – powered medical app. Our aim in developing such a product was to:
- Access to modern technology: AI is a technology trend that is applied a lot in life and researches a lot for the future. AI was assumed to simulate human reasoning, giving the ability of a computer program to learn and think. Such a man-made anthropomorphic intelligence has been developed as simple rule-based systems, symbolic logical general AI, and sub-symbolic statistical narrow AI. Such an AI is an automation technology that emulates human performance, typically by learning from it, to enhance existing applications and processes. Besides, AI can manage medical records and quickly enter user data. Instead of waiting for an employee to have to look at thousands of numbers and enter each number into each patient’s record, AI can handle this problem quickly and not keep users waiting for long. Currently, there are many applications that use Ai to provide medical advice based on personal medical history and common medical knowledge. Patients can report their symptoms into the app, which then uses voice recognition to compare with existing disease databases. The app will then provide suggested actions to the user’s medical account history. The AI system was created to help select the right treatment.
- Convince for travel: This app is very handy, especially for those who live in the suburbs or in the provinces. When they or a loved one is sick, it takes them a long distance to go to a hospital in the city to see the illness. It is in a sick state but if the disease is not serious but also needs proper treatment, it takes time to go that far. This medical app is a solution to those problems. When users are not seriously ill, they can diagnose the disease through AI on the app to know how they need to be treated.
- Can view the disease 24/24: Unlike the hourly staff, our medical app works 24/24 even on public holidays to be able to consult and treat patients at all times.
- User-friendly and gives the best advice: The AI staff of our medical app is very friendly and dedicated to users. Depending on the severity of the disease, the AI staff will advise in detail whether to treat at home or not. When treated at home, the treatment methods that the AI staff recommend are based on medicine and science to help the patient recover the fastest and best. Furthermore, the phone’s webcam is partnered with AI that will automatically confirm when a patient is taking prescription medication and help them manage their condition.

4) Scope and Limits


Project Scope


- A medical app that helps and keeps track of people’s health in the midst of Covid-19:
+ A login system: each user will have a separate account,
+ A main page showing a brief summary of the user’s record,
+ A user profile: it will have everything from past medical records and all the treatments,
+ Vaccination information: a page linking with PC-Covid’s data to show the user their vaccination record,
+ Location saver: a list of locations the user has gone to and scanned using PC-Covid app,
+ Diseases’ information: every information about known diseases for the user to look up.
+ An appointment scheduling: a page recording all the appointment schedule and upcoming appointment for the user,
+ A prescription record: a record for prescriptions that the user can use and go to a local pharmacist or a hospital for the medicines.
- A chatbot integrated with AI that will accompany patients and ask about their current health:
+ 24/7 chatbot: user can talk to a chatbot about their health,
+ While talking to the bot, it will ask the user questions about their current health. And based on the responses, it will tell the user what the problem is, what kind of self-treatment is recommended and whether or not a doctor consulting is needed,
+ When a consultant is needed, an appointment will be made and recorded for ease of use for the user.


Out of Scope


- We will not have a payment system for the prescription and appointment,
- This project’s initial launch plan will only be available in Vietnam. We do have plans to launch it globally.


Project deliverables


The complete app project will include:
+ A private individual record for each patient recording their health,
+ An appointment and prescription record,
+ A user profile: it will have everything from past medical records and all the treatments,
+ A chatbot with AI integration asking patients of their health and giving medical recommendation accordingly based of our AI training,
+ A prescription holder that can be written by the consulting doctor.

Limits and constraints


- Location service: it is required that the user scan the location using PC-Covid app, but if they don’t scan it, then no location will be recorded
- Doctor’s monitoring: our project is still limited and needs our team monitoring alongside the doctor’s assessment in order to keep everything correct. We are planning to give it some update so the doctor can do everything with little help from the team.
- AI’s efficiency: because the chat bot is using an AI, the early stages of the app will most definitely give some weird responses (but we will make sure to limit this problem). And as time goes on, the AI will get better and give more precise judgements.
